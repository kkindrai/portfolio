Thank you for your interest in Willow Gardens! Below you will find information on our plots, our pricing plans, and a form to apply for your future plot!

About Our Plots

At Willow Gardens, we strive to ensure your gardening experience the easiest and most fulfilling possible.

General Information

  • Size: Our Garden Plots Measure approximately 20' x 20' (400sq. ft.).
  • Quantity: The maximum number of plots per gardener is 4 plots.
  • Allowed Methods: Only organic gardening methods are allowed.
  • Tilling: We do not till any sections of our gardens which allows gardeners to plant perennials such as asparagus and strawberries and leave up fences, if desired, throughout the year.

Available Provisions

  • Water is provided on site throughout the season.
  • Garden tools are available for public use.
  • Free woodchips are provided for gardener use.
  • We offer hay and row cover for sale on our application.

Plot Pricing

$15, $43, or $78 per plot, per year, depending on your income level, size of your household, and or garden family.
